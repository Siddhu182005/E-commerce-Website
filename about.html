Our Story

Born from
Obsession.

LUMIS began in 2021 in a Copenhagen basement, where three audio engineers refused to accept compromise. What started as a side project became a mission: make the world's finest headphone available to everyone who deserves great sound.

4 Years of R&D
1,200+ Prototypes Built
48 Expert Listeners
3 Founders

Sound is Not
Negotiable.

We believe that audio is the most underserved category in consumer technology. Brands compete on features and price wars, forgetting that the fundamental question is simple: does it sound extraordinary?

At LUMIS, we start with acoustics. We spend months on driver geometry before touching industrial design. We tune every ANC algorithm by ear, in real environments — not just anechoic chambers. Our final approval is always this: does it give us chills?

If the answer isn't an immediate yes, we start over.

How We Got Here

The Journey

2021

The Basement Lab

Three acoustic engineers from B&O and Sennheiser leave their jobs. Prototype #1 is built in a Copenhagen basement. It sounds terrible. They start again.

2022

Liquid Crystal Discovery

Prototype #134 introduces a liquid-crystal polymer diaphragm. The team listens back-to-back for 6 hours. Nobody wants to stop. They know they have something.

2023

Seed Funding & Team Growth

€2.4M raised from audio-focused investors. The team grows to 12. The ANC algorithm is rebuilt 7 times. Prototype #847 passes the hallway test — strangers ask what they're wearing.

2024

Manufacturing Partnership

Partnership signed with a Taiwanese precision manufacturer. First production run: 500 units. Sent to 48 audio professionals worldwide. Average score: 9.4/10.

2025

LUMIS Pro X Launch

LUMIS Pro X ships worldwide. The first review calls it "the headphone Sony and Bose should be afraid of." We agree.

What Drives Us

Our
Values

01

Sound First

Every decision — materials, components, algorithms — starts and ends with how it affects the listening experience. Features that don't serve sound don't ship.

02

Ruthless Craft

We built 1,200+ prototypes before shipping one. That's not inefficiency — that's the only way to make something that deserves to exist.

03

Radical Transparency

We publish our full frequency response charts. We don't tune for showroom demos. What you hear in your home is what we measured in our lab.

04

Longevity by Design

Every LUMIS is built to last a decade. Replaceable ear cushions, detachable cables, software-updatable firmware. We hate planned obsolescence.
