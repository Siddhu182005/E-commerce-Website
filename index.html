LUMIS Pro X is the result of four years of acoustic engineering. Our proprietary liquid-crystal polymer diaphragm delivers frequency response from 4Hz to 40kHz — well beyond what competitors offer. Pair this with our adaptive 6-mic ANC system and you get silence on demand, music on command.

  • LDAC / AAC / SBC codec support
